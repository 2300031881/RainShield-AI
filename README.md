#  RainShield AI
AI-powered parametric insurance for food delivery workers

---

## Problem
Delivery workers from platforms like Zomato and Swiggy rely heavily on daily earnings. However, their income is highly vulnerable to external disruptions such as heavy rainfall, extreme weather, and environmental conditions.

Studies show that gig workers can lose **20–30% of their income** due to such uncontrollable factors. Currently, there is no insurance solution that protects their **loss of income**, leaving them financially unstable during these events.

---

##  Solution
RainShield AI is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed specifically to protect delivery workers from income loss.

The platform:
- Monitors real-time environmental conditions  
- Detects disruptions like heavy rainfall  
- Automatically triggers insurance claims  
- Instantly compensates workers without manual intervention  

 This ensures **zero-touch, fast, and reliable financial protection**.

---

##  Target Users
- Food delivery riders (Zomato, Swiggy)  
- Workers dependent on daily/weekly income  
- Urban gig workers affected by weather disruptions  

---

##  Workflow

1. User registers with basic details (name, city, platform)  
2. System assigns a risk profile using AI  
3. Weekly premium is calculated dynamically  
4. Platform continuously monitors weather data (via APIs)  
5. If rainfall exceeds threshold (e.g., >20mm):  
   - Disruption is detected  
   - Claim is triggered automatically  
6. Payout is processed instantly to the user  

Entire process is **automated with no manual claim filing**

---

## Weekly Pricing Model

The pricing is designed based on risk levels:

- **Low Risk Areas** → ₹20/week (Coverage up to ₹500)  
- **Medium Risk Areas** → ₹35/week (Coverage up to ₹1000)  
- **High Risk Areas** → ₹50/week (Coverage up to ₹1500)  

Pricing factors include:
- Historical rainfall data  
- Location-based risk  
- Frequency of disruptions  

 This aligns with gig workers’ **weekly earning cycle**

---

## Parametric Triggers

RainShield AI uses predefined conditions to trigger payouts automatically.

### Environmental Triggers:
- Rainfall > 20mm  
- Extreme weather alerts  
- Flood warnings  

### Impact:
- Reduced working hours  
- Fewer deliveries  
- Loss of income  

Once triggered, payout is automatic (no verification delay)

---

## AI Usage

AI is used to enhance efficiency and accuracy:

- **Risk-Based Premium Calculation**  
  → Adjusts pricing based on location and weather patterns  

- **Income Loss Prediction**  
  → Estimates potential earnings loss during disruptions  

- **Fraud Detection**  
  → Identifies abnormal patterns and prevents misuse  

---

## Fraud Detection

To ensure system reliability:

- GPS location verification  
- Duplicate claim prevention  
- Behavior pattern analysis  
- Detection of unrealistic activity

##  Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React (for user interface)  
- **Backend:** Node.js or SpringBoot (API and logic handling)  
- **Database:** MongoDB or SQL (for storing users and claims)  
- **API Integration:** OpenWeather API (for real-time weather data)  
- **Payments:** Razorpay (test mode for payout simulation)
